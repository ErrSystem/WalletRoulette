Wallet Roulette

+ Introducing Wallet Roulette, a cutting-edge utility designed to revolutionize the way you find the forgotten and hidden crypto treasures. Wallet Roulette goes beyond conventional tools by rolling existing wallet private keys and give an easy access to the coin holdings across a diverse range of networks. This innovative solution is your ticket to unlocking the ultimate crypto jackpot!

How To Use:

+ First you'll need to lunch the app by click on the button bellow.
+ Then you'll have to pay for a number of RLTs that are considered as tickets.
+ After paying you'll have to choose between Manual spin or Automatic spin.
+ Manual spin: You'll have to click or scroll down every time you want to roll.
+ Automatic spin: The app will automaticly spin for you until it finds something or you run out of RLTs.
+ Every time you spin you'll be able to see the private key, the public key, the total balance and the balance in each network / blockchain supported!
